About Me

Curious about
money & the world

I'm Souvik Das — a writer and thinker based in Kolkata, India. My curiosity lives at the intersection of economics, financial psychology, and the everyday decisions that quietly determine the quality of our lives.

I write because I believe that financial literacy shouldn't be reserved for economists or bankers. The ideas I explore here are meant for anyone who wants to think more clearly about money, markets, and their own future.

When I'm not writing, I'm reading — history, behavioural economics, philosophy, and anything that challenges the way I see the world.
