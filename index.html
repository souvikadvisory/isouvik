Who I Am

About Me

01

I'm Souvik Das — a finance professional, writer, and advisor based in Kolkata. I work at the intersection of money, economics, and human behaviour, helping individuals and businesses navigate the complex world of financial decision-making.

Currently serving as Assistant Manager at Axis Max Life Insurance Limited, I lead team performance, business expansion strategy, investment planning, and client relationship management. Previously, I contributed to HDB Financial Services Ltd and IDFC First Bank.

Academically, I am pursuing an MA in Economics from IGNOU alongside a B.Com in Business Management & Finance from Calcutta University. My long-term vision is to contribute meaningfully in investment management, economic research, or senior banking leadership — while achieving financial independence.

Beyond my professional life, I write about economics, behavioural finance, and the ideas that shape our world. I believe financial literacy is not a privilege — it should be accessible to everyone.
